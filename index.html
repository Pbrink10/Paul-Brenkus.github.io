WELCOME!

Hello everyone and welcome to my page. My name is Paul Brenkus and I am a Business Analytics and Data Science student at the College of William & Mary. I started this page for multiple reasons. First, I wanted this to be a hub for other students just like me who want to pursue careers in analytics, data science, or machine learning. Here they can look at the code and algorithms that I have written and hopefully get some inspiration or provide me with their opinion on my work. Second, I am a veteran, and as many other veterans know, navigating the world outside of the service can be tricky, so hopefully this site can help guide others like me on a path towards a great career. I hope that you enjoy exploring this site and please feel free to reach out anytime.

Interests

As a full-time student, I am constantly learning new platforms and tools to strive in field of Business Analytics and Data Science. Some of those programs include: Tableau, Python, R, HTML, and SQL. I tend to be the type where my brain needs to continuously stimulated with new material and I find that these programs, amongst others, give me plenty. I am looking to expand on my machine learning capibilities such as facial recognition in sports, simulation predictions amongst sporting teams, airport security item identification technology, as well as childrens interactive machine learning activities. If anyone has any ideas of their own, please feel free to shoot them out to me and I will hopefully help to achieve your goals.

Hobbies

Like I said in the previous paragraph, I am full-time student in a high demanding program, but that doesn't keep me from having some type of life. On my down time, I enjoy spending time with my wife (Kristin), my son (Lincoln), and my dog (Arya). We enjoy going for walks near Yorktown beach, random car rides around town, and the occasional TJ-Maxx trip. You can always find me looking up some type of information on sports, specifically hockey.
